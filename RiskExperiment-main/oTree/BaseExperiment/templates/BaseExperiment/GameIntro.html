1. For the first round, the supervisor will not allocate the production time to managers and each regional manager will be given 5 minutes of production time to make sandwiches.

2. The regional managers make as many sandwiches as they can in the time given.

3. Once the time is over, the regional managers will be informed about the sales performance they have achieved in this round. Furthermore, they receive a local market survey report, conducted in their shop during this round. As it is locally conducted, this survey report is available only to the shopâ€™s regional manager, not to others.

4. Then the regional managers will send a report of the sales performance they achieved to the supervisor. The supervisor will receive the reports and subsequently allocate 10 minutes of production time between two regional managers for the next round. Each regional manager can make sandwiches only during the production time the supervisor will allocate to him/her in the next round.

The game will continue with steps 2,3, and 4 for the next rounds.
